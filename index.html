Smash Up Drafter

Welcome to Smash Up Drafter

Fast and Simple: pick the sets you own and start drafting!

Getting Started

  1. Select Your Sets

    Choose all the sets you currently own (open settings).

  2. Build Your Draft

    Hit “Draft” to get your faction selection.

  3. Play and Smash!

    Your draft is ready — time to battle and have fun!

How Drafting Works

The draft is based only on the sets you’ve marked as owned.

Each player gets one faction, then picks a second from the remaining ones.

To draft, you need at least one core set (not only expansions).
